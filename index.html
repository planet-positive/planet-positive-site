PlanetPositive.tez

Our mission is to harness the Tezos community as a positive force for nurturing and protecting the natural planet.

Our Goals

Our very first goal is to make sure that the Tezos network captures at least 100x more CO2 than is released through the energy usage of the nodes that run it. An independent analysis by PwC estimated that the total annual carbon footprint for Tezos in 2021 approximates the average footprint of 17 world citizens. We'd like to offset the necessary emissions resulting from this, while also supporting developments that drive the network energy usage lower as part of regular protocol upgrades.

Our Approach

We are creating a Tezos multisig wallet that will buy high-integrity carbon credits for nature-based solutions and retire them immediately towards the Tezos network's necessary emissions.

Anyone in the community can contribute to the wallet: projects, bakers, holders, dapps. It is about taking collective responsibility and action to make Tezos a force for tremendous good in the natural world. Everyone involved in the Tezos community is welcome to participate: it's not just the people that run indexes or bakers who are responsible for the emissions, but all the users who benefit from the network as well.

We will deploy remote sensing MRV technology to assess which carbon credits we buy to ensure maximum and verifiable impact. Our priority will be on projects that use Tezos to implement on-chain verification and reproducibility.

Each month we will submit a proposal to buy a specific mix of carbon credits from different sources, together with the evidence and reasoning for buying that mix from those suppliers. When the signatories agree, the carbon credits will be bought and retired.
